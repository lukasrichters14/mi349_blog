About Lukas

Lukas is a senior computer science major at Michigan State University. He will be graduating with a bachelor's of science in computer science in May 2022. After graduation, he will pursue a master's degree at MSU while working for KLA. Outside of CS, Lukas is involved in many extra-curriculars at MSU, including:

  • Teaching Assistant for Algorithms and Data Structures

  • Co-director of SpartaHack

  • Research Assitant for Dr. Guan-Hua Tu

  • Director of Technology for Spartan Thrift Club

Lukas' primary interests in his field are:

  • Embedded and low-level computer systems

  • Computer networking

  • Computer security

During his limited free time, you can find Lukas with friends and family, practicing guitar, and losing at chess.
